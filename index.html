what we offer

Educational programs on the Holy Quran

Our educational programs on the Holy Quran are available for all ages and genders (men, women, and children).

Programs for children

We offer classes and lessons tailored for children (suitable for children under 16 years of age).

Private programs for men & women

Qur'an courses and classes suitable for adult men and women through one-on-one lessons.

Assistance in learning to read and write

People of the Qur'an academy provides a special section for those who have difficulty understanding and reading.

What makes our programs unique

Our programs are unique in that they start from a level of non-proficiency in reading and progress to a level of mastery in reading, reciting, and chanting the Holy Quran.

Plans for memorizing the Quran

We set different timetables for memorizing the Holy Quran to suit each student's abilities and age.

why choose us

We are distinguished by offering individual lessons, experienced teachers, ample study time anywhere, and solutions for children with memorization and communication problems in limited languages.

Taking individual differences into account We offer one-on-one lessons between the teacher and the student, allowing the student to progress at their own pace.

Our teachers are highly proficient in Arabic, having graduated from Al-Azhar University and memorized the Holy Quran.

If your child is not fluent in Arabic, we have teachers who are fluent in the following languages (English, German, Spanish, French, Italian) and can communicate with and teach your child Arabic from scratch to fluency.

Our teachers are able to apply scientific solutions to children's learning problems online, including shyness, unwillingness to participate in lessons, lack of enthusiasm, and excessive movement during class.
